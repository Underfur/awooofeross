How commissions work

Age requirement: Individuals must be 18+ to request or purchase adult-themed commissions.

Slots & updates: Availability is posted on social media. If you want a commission, contact via the links below.

Payments: Payment is required upfront before work begins. Confirmation will be sent via the chosen platform (FurAffinity, Telegram, X/Twitter, Bluesky or Discord).

Refunds & cancellations: Request cancellations as soon as possible — generally no refunds once work has started.

Turnaround: Typical completion time is ~1 week to 1 month depending on queue and complexity. If you need a strict deadline, an additional fee may apply.

Drawing process

  1. Rough sketch provided for approval — request reasonable changes at this stage.
  2. After approval and final work, only minor edits are accepted (colors, small markings).
  3. Final delivery: high-quality PNG file. Artist may post the piece in their gallery.
